<Section icon={Target} title="How to Play Narmaya’s Rotation">

Narmaya performs best when you treat her like a timing character, not a freestyle attacker. Build momentum with safe strings, then cash in during boss windows. When the fight slows down, you can press your advantage; when the boss starts moving, reset your spacing and wait for the next opening.

The cleanest loop is repetitive on purpose. Good Narmaya play is less about improvising every second and more about recognizing when a window is worth spending resources on.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open with safe attacks">
    Start with your most reliable normal strings. Build pressure without committing your biggest tools too early.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Save skills for clear openings">
    Use skills when the boss is locked, staggered, or finishing a long animation. That keeps your damage efficient.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spend bursts in the right window">
    Pair your strongest actions with Link Attacks, Chain Bursts, or extended boss downtime. That is where Narmaya feels strongest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset when the fight becomes chaotic">
    If the boss starts spinning, teleporting, or flooding the field, stop forcing damage and reposition cleanly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Repeat the pattern consistently">
    The goal is repeatable pressure. Stable execution will usually outscore flashy but risky improvisation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
