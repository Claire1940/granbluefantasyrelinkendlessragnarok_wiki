<Section icon={Target} title="The A-Tier Damage Dealers (Above Average)">

A-Tier characters are highly effective and capable of carrying you through all endgame content. They do not possess the inherently "broken" safety mechanics of S-Tier units, but their damage output is excellent when played with proper execution.

- **Zeta**: Focuses on looping high-altitude aerial attacks. She mixes offense and defense naturally by staying airborne, though she requires precise timing to sustain her loops.
- **Narmaya**: Features fast stance-swapping combos and massive burst potential. Her unique butterfly resource does not decay when she stops attacking, allowing her to easily maintain high DPS.
- **Siegfried**: A rhythm-based attacker who relies on perfectly timed combo finishers. He can dodge mid-combo without resetting his sequence, providing consistent high-damage output.
- **Percival**: Relies on charging his powerful Schlacht skill. He is easy to play, boasts built-in supplemental damage, and serves as an excellent AI companion.
- **Eugen**: The premier ranged DPS. He can safely destroy bosses from afar with high-damage sniper shots and grenade setups, making him the top-ranked AI teammate.

<Section icon={Shield} title="The B-Tier & C-Tier Damage Dealers (Average to Low)">

Characters in the B and C tiers often suffer from overly complex mechanics, low damage multipliers, or a design focus that prioritizes utility over raw damage. In a meta where boss fights feature one-shot mechanics, raw speed and damage often outweigh defensive utility.

### B-Tier: Average but Complex
These characters can deal respectable damage, but you must work twice as hard to achieve the same results as an S-Tier character.
- **Ghandagoza**: Relies on building his Raging Meter to unleash devastating fist strikes. However, his meter drains rapidly if he stops attacking, making mobile bosses a nightmare to fight.
- **Catalina**: Requires building her Ares gauge to summon her guardian for enhanced attacks. If she is forced to dodge or block, the gauge resets, severely punishing mistakes.
- **Lancelot**: Excellent for freezing bosses and spamming SBA, but his individual strike damage is moderate.

### C-Tier: Utility & Low Damage
These characters provide great team buffs but fall behind significantly on the damage charts.
- **Ferry**: Requires keeping her spectral pets active to deal optimal damage. Using her strongest skills consumes these pets, leading to frequent DPS downtime.
- **Rosetta**: Relies on placing roses around the battlefield. Mobile bosses quickly move away from her roses, and her summons do not benefit from supplementary damage effects.
- **Tweyen**: A fantastic support unit capable of inflicting status ailments and breaking boss parts. However, her raw DPS is low, and major endgame bosses like Lucilius are immune to most of her debuffs.

| Character | Primary Drawback | Utility Provided |
|---|---|---|
| **Ghandagoza** | Meter decays quickly when not hitting boss | High stun value |
| **Catalina** | Ares gauge resets upon dodging/blocking | Group Invincibility (Light Wall) |
| **Ferry** | High DPS downtime due to pet management | Decent SBA gauge generation |
| **Rosetta** | Stationary roses fail against mobile bosses | Group defensive and offensive buffs |
| **Tweyen** | Low damage multipliers; debuffs fail on bosses | Excellent crowd control and part breaking |

<Callout type="warning" title="Endgame Reality">
Characters like Rosetta and Tweyen struggle significantly in fights like Lucilius due to boss immunities and high mobility, making them less optimal for speed farming.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Optimize Your DPS">

To bridge the gap between tiers and ensure your favorite character can tackle the toughest raids, follow this step-by-step progression path to optimize your build.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Max Out Weapons & Masteries">
    Prioritize upgrading your character's ascension or Terminus weapon to level 150. Complete 100% of their offensive and defensive mastery trees to unlock essential base stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Damage Cap Sigils">
    Damage in Relink is strictly limited by damage caps. Equip Damage Cap sigils to raise this ceiling, ensuring your attacks deal their maximum potential damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incorporate Supplementary Damage">
    Equip Supplementary Damage sigils. These grant your attacks a chance to deal an extra instance of damage that bypasses the standard damage cap, drastically raising your overall DPS.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master Perfect Dodging">
    Learn boss attack patterns to trigger perfect evades regularly. This grants temporary invincibility, allowing you to maintain your offensive uptime without interrupting your combos.
  </Step>
</Steps>
