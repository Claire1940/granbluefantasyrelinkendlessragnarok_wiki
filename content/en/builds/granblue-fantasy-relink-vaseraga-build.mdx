<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Combo Techniques and Dodge Canceling">

Maximizing DPS in a **granblue fantasy relink Vaseraga build** requires mastering the "Dodge Cancel." Vaseraga's standard animations are slow, but you can skip the recovery frames of his light attacks to reach his high-damage finishers faster.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Basic Gap Closer">
    If **Battalions of Fear** is on cooldown, perform a light attack followed immediately by a heavy attack. This creates a lunging gap closer that leads directly into a combo finisher.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Light-Light-Heavy-Heavy (LLHH) Combo">
    The LLHH combo deals significantly more damage than the longer LLLHH chain. Focus on this for stationary targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Dodge Cancel Trick">
    Perform the first light attack, then immediately dodge to cancel the animation. Perform the second light attack and dodge again. You can then immediately transition into the massive charged heavy finisher.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Infinite Charging">
    After a combo finisher, dodge immediately. You can then begin charging the next heavy attack instantly, bypassing the neutral state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Timing Warning">
Canceling too early will prevent the hit from registering. Practice the rhythm in the training area until the dodge occurs exactly as the scythe makes contact with the enemy.
</Callout>

</Section>
