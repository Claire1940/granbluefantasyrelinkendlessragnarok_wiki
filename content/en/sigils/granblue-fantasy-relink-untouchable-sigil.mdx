<Section icon={Shield} title="Untouchable vs. Other Defensive Sigils">

Many players wonder if the **granblue fantasy relink untouchable sigil** is worth a slot over pure survival traits like Aegis or Autorevive. The answer depends on your skill level and character choice. Untouchable is a "proactive" defensive sigil—it helps you avoid damage entirely—whereas Aegis and Guts are "reactive" sigils that help you survive damage you've already taken.

| Sigil Name | Primary Function | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Untouchable** | Increases Dodge Distance | Melee DPS, Mobility, Avoiding AOE |
| **Aegis** | Increases Max HP | Tanks, Surviving One-Shots |
| **Guts** | Survive fatal hit with 1 HP | Learning new boss patterns |
| **Autorevive** | Automatic Resurrection | Reliability in long endurance fights |

In the 2026 meta, the "glass cannon" approach is dominant. Players prefer to use Untouchable and Improved Dodge to stay mobile and maintain their Damage Cap uptime. If you can dodge an attack entirely, you don't need the extra HP from Aegis. This allows you to slot in more offensive traits like Tyranny or Stamina.

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Analysis">
Melee characters like Lancelot or Charlotta benefit more from Untouchable than ranged characters like Io or Rackam. Ranged characters are usually far enough away that extra dodge distance is redundant, whereas melee units need it to escape point-blank explosions.
</Callout>
