<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Mechanics: Avatar Gauge and Swordshine">

Seofon's playstyle functions as a high-speed Bruiser. His user interface features a dual-gauge system that dictates his damage output. Managing these resources correctly is the foundation of any successful **granblue fantasy relink seofon build**.

| Gauge Component | Purpose | How to Build |
|-----------------|---------|--------------|
| **Avatar Gauge** (Left) | Dictates the duration of your summoned Avatar. | Basic combos, Link Attacks, Tempesta skill. |
| **Swordshine Level** (Right) | Increases Avatar hit counts and unlocks the ultimate finisher. | Combos while Avatar is active, Skills, Link Attacks. |

To maximize your Avatar Gauge efficiently, you must use the correct basic combo string.

| Combo Input | Gauge Build Efficiency | Optimal Use Case |
|-------------|------------------------|------------------|
| 1 Light -> Heavy | Very Low (11 combos to max) | Not recommended |
| 2 Light -> 2 Heavy | Moderate (7 combos to max) | Decent for quick repositioning |
| 3 Light -> 2 Heavy | Good (6 combos to max) | Solid filler |
| 4 Light -> 1 Heavy | Excellent (5 combos to max) | Best standard gauge builder |

<Callout type="warning" title="Conserve Your Gauge">
You can manually dismiss your Avatar by pressing the heavy attack button again. Doing this before the Avatar Gauge completely empties conserves the remaining meter for your next opening.
</Callout>

Your gear choices make or break your performance in Proud-difficulty raids like Lucilius or Behemoth. 

### Weapon Progression
Seofon only has two weapons available. You should prioritize fully upgrading his **Ascension Weapon**. At maximum level, it grants built-in Supplementary Damage and Sigil Booster, raising all your equipped sigil trait levels by 1.

### Recommended Sigil Loadout

| Sigil Name | Primary Effect | Why It Is Vital |
|------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Seven Star Boundary** | +50% ATK, +100% Damage Cap, caps HP at 45,000 | This is Seofon's version of the Terminus passive. |
| **Spirit Edges War Path** | Grants Stout Heart, Drain, Debuff Immunity, and +20% Enhanced Damage | Triggers automatically after executing an Avatar Finisher. |
| **Spirit Edges Fury** | +30% ATK, +30% Stun Power, +15% Damage Cap | Flat damage cap increase with no negative penalties. |
| **Spirit Edges Rally** | +12.5% Swordshine Level per skill activated | Allows you to reach max Swordshine instantly using skills. |

<Callout type="success" title="Sigil Transmutation Tip">
You can obtain the Awakening version of Seofon's unique sigils at the Knickknack Shack, which combines Spirit Edges Rally and Fury into a single slot, freeing up room for more utility.
</Callout>

### Supporting Sigils Setup
To round out your offensive and defensive capabilities, aim for the following supporting sigils:

- **War Elemental**: Converts all attacks to the enemy's weak element (+20% damage).
- **Tyranny & Stamina**: Easily caps your raw physical damage.
- **Lucilius Sigils (Alpha/Beta/Gamma)**: Further boosts damage caps.
- **Supplementary Damage**: Aim for a high level to trigger extra damage hits frequently.

To play Seofon at his highest potential, you must execute a specific combat loop that maximizes his unique buffs.

  <Step num="2" title="Gap Close and Stun">

  <Step num="4" title="Execute Finisher">

  </Step>
</Steps>
