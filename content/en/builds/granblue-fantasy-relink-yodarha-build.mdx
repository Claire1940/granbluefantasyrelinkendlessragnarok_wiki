### The Combo Finisher Loop
Executing four consecutive combo finishers shortens your basic attack string [1]. This allows you to perform a full combo with a single button press, letting you spam finishers rapidly [1]. Because Yodarha is invincible during the multiple slashes of his combo finisher, this loop provides unmatched survivability while sustaining high damage output [1].

### Shroud Marks (Stamps)
Performing a combo finisher or a Link Attack followed by a finisher awards Shroud Marks (up to a maximum of three) [1]. These marks drastically enhance the damage, duration, or utility of Yodarha's active skills [1].

### The Heavy Lunge and Counter Parry
Holding Yodarha's heavy attack button puts him in a blocking stance [1]. Releasing the button right before an enemy attack hits triggers a precise parry [1]. A successful parry grants a temporary **Supplementary Damage** buff (dealing an extra 25% of the original hit's damage) for 10 seconds [1]. Mastering this timing separates average players from elite Yodarha masters [1].

| Mechanic | Input / Action | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Combo Finisher** | End of light attack string | Grants **Invincibility** frames and 1 Shroud Mark [1] |
| **Infinite Loop** | 4 successful finishers | Shortens combo string to a single button press [1] |
| **Link Attack Follow-up** | Link Attack -> Finisher | Instantly generates 2 Shroud Marks [1] |
| **Counter Parry** | Release Heavy Attack on flash | Grants **Supplementary Damage** for 10 seconds [1] |

<Callout type="tip" title="Parry Optimization">
The parry window is indicated by a white circular flash on Yodarha [1]. Releasing the button exactly during this flash guarantees the counter [1]. If you miss the timing, you can quickly dodge out of the animation to avoid taking damage [2].

Choosing the right active skills is crucial to maximizing your damage output and providing vital utility to your team [1]. The optimal endgame setup focuses on high burst damage, cooldown management, and team-wide survivability [1, 2].

### Active Skills Breakdown

*   **Empty Mist**: Yodarha's premier damage skill [1]. Holding down the button unleashes rapid slashes that scale in duration based on your Shroud Marks [1]. This skill is incredibly potent against large bosses with multiple hitboxes [1].
*   **Perpetual Rotation**: The cornerstone of Yodarha's rotation [1, 2]. If used with zero marks, it instantly grants all three Shroud Marks [1]. If used when you already have three marks, it instantly resets all of Yodarha's active skill cooldowns [1].
*   **Flashing Void**: A high-damage gap closer that requires rhythmic button presses [1]. Pressing the attack button in sync with the glowing white circles extends the combo up to 12 times, dealing massive damage [1]. You can move during this animation to adjust your positioning [1].
*   **Hymn of the Hundreds**: Grants Yodarha the **Mirror Image** buff, allowing him to ignore the next three hits [1]. When activated at three Shroud Marks, this buff is applied to the entire party, making it an invaluable tool for surviving major boss mechanics [1].

### Weapon Progression

1.  **Stinger Weapon (Fushirin)**: The best choice for early-to-mid progression [1]. It provides a massive boost to critical hit rate, helping you hit damage caps consistently before obtaining endgame gear [1].
