<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Combo Sequences">

Executing the perfect rotation with **granblue fantasy relink vane** ensures your Beatdown gauge stays full and your cooldowns remain low. Landing a finisher is not just about damage; it is your primary source of healing and gauge generation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gauge Building">
    Perform the basic 3-hit combo (Square, Square, Square) followed by the Triangle finisher. This is the fastest way to build your initial Beatdown meter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Skill Integration">
    Use **Heroic Beat** to close the distance or **Arm Destruction** to apply heavy stun. Immediately follow any offensive skill with a Triangle finisher to gain bonus gauge and health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Beatdown Burst">
    Once the gauge is full, press Triangle to start the Beatdown combo. Hold the button to continue the chain. Remember, you are blocking during this entire sequence!
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defensive Utility">
    Watch the boss's telegraphs. When a massive AOE is imminent, activate **Rampart**. Stand still to maintain the dome and allow your teammates to hide inside for total invincibility.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Link Time Mechanics">
During Link Time, Vane can spam his Triangle attacks without consuming the Beatdown gauge. This allows for infinite blocking and massive sustained DPS for the duration of the effect.
</Callout>
