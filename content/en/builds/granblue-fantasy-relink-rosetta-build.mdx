<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Skills for Maximum DPS and Utility">

Selecting the right skills is the most critical part of an optimized **granblue fantasy relink rosetta build**. While Rosetta has several support-oriented skills, her endgame meta revolves around a specific interaction between **Iron Maiden** and **Spiral Rose**.

| Skill Name | Role | Why It Is Used |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Spiral Rose** | Main DPS | Spawns vines from Rosetta and all roses; hits dozens of times in seconds. |
| **Iron Maiden** | Buff/Enabler | Grants Stout Heart and adds extra rose attacks to every hit Rosetta makes. |
| **Rose Tycoon** | Utility | Repositions all roses to the target and applies a 1.5% Max HP poison. |
| **Rose Barrier** | Defense | Provides up to 40% damage cut based on rose level; vital for survivability. |
| **Lost Love** | Burst | Explodes all roses for massive damage; roses are instantly replanted after. |

The "Secret Tech" for Rosetta involves activating Iron Maiden before using Spiral Rose. Because Spiral Rose creates many individual hitboxes originating from Rosetta's model, Iron Maiden triggers an additional rose attack for every single one of those hits. This results in a "shredding" effect that can delete boss HP bars, especially during Link Time.

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Combo Finishers and Buff Cycles">

Rosetta provides three distinct buffs to the entire party through her combo finishers. The potency of these buffs scales with the highest rose level currently on the field. In a completed **granblue fantasy relink rosetta build**, you will almost always have Level 4 roses, providing 20% bonuses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Buff Color">
    Watch the circle under Rosetta as you perform basic attacks. Yellow indicates Defense, Blue indicates Regen, and Pink indicates Attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defense Buff (1 Attack + Finisher)">
    Perform one basic swing followed by the finisher. This is the most valuable endgame buff as it helps the team survive chip damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Regen Buff (2 Attacks + Finisher)">
    Perform two swings then the finisher. Useful during downtime or transition phases where the boss is untargetable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Attack Buff (3 Attacks + Finisher)">
    Perform three swings then the finisher. Only use this if your teammates are struggling to hit the damage cap.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Buff Type | Color | Effect at Level 4 | Strategic Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Defense** | Yellow | +20% Defense | Default for most endgame raids. |
| **Regen** | Blue | Passive HP Recovery | Best for "heal-check" mechanics. |
| **Attack** | Pink | +20% Attack | Use for teammates with low-investment builds. |

<Callout type="success" title="Buff Retention">
Using the skill **Lost Love** to explode your roses does not remove the active buff. The newly replanted roses will automatically inherit the last buff you applied, saving you from having to restart your combo cycle immediately.
</Callout>
