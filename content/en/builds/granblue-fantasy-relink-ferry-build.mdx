<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Skills and Ability Rotations">

Choosing the right skills is critical for balancing Ferry's damage and support capabilities. In the current 2026 meta, offensive skills that maintain pet uptime are favored over pure defensive options.

| Skill Name | Primary Effect | Pet Requirement | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hinrichten** | Atk/Crit/Supp Damage/Invincible | 1 to 4 Pets | Use at 4 pets for maximum DPS burst |
| **Purge Spirits** | High Stagger + Summon All Pets | None | Primary tool for triggering Link Attacks |
| **Umlauf** | Beppo AOE + Stout Heart | None | Essential for uninterrupted aerial combos |
| **Strife** | Summon All Pets + High Stagger | None | Quick pet recovery after Onslaught |
| **Benediction** | Regen/Def Up/Atk Up | 1 to 3 Pets | Utility support for early Proud difficulty |

**Hinrichten** is the cornerstone of her damage. When cast with four pets, it provides a massive 50% Attack boost, 30% Critical Hit rate, and Supplementary Damage. The invincibility window is a lifesaver during chaotic boss phases.

<Callout type="warning" title="Endgame Buff Scaling">

<Section icon={Target} title="The 30 Million Damage Aerial Tech">

While many players rely on the **Onslaught** (heavy attack) finisher, the highest DPS for a **granblue fantasy relink ferry build** actually comes from her aerial playstyle. This method bypasses the pet-loss penalty of Onslaught while maintaining a high damage ceiling.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup">
    Cast **Strife** and immediately dodge-cancel it. This puts all pets on the field without the long animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Buffing Up">
    Activate **Umlauf** for Stout Heart and **Hinrichten** for Supplementary Damage. Dodge-cancel these as well to stay mobile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Launcher">
    Use your launcher attack (Jump + Triangle/Y) to get into the air.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Aerial Barrage Spam">
    Repeatedly use the Aerial Barrage. It has the same damage cap as Onslaught but allows your pets to keep attacking independently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Link Attack Integration">
    When a Link Attack triggers, use it to instantly refresh your pets and continue the loop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Stout Heart Synergy">
By using Umlauf to gain Stout Heart, Ferry becomes uninterruptible. This allows you to stay in the air and maintain your barrage even if the boss attempts to swat you away with minor attacks.
</Callout>

</Section>
