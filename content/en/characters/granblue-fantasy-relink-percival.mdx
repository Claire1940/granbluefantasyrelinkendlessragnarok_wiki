  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Crewmate Cards">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Recruit at Siero's Shack">

### Advanced Tech Breakdown

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Schlacht Charging">
    Percival's secondary attack, **Schlacht**, is a chargeable heavy strike. Normally, it leaves him vulnerable, but using skills beforehand drastically increases its charge speed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dodge Canceling">
    Activate a skill like **Royal Authority** or **Macht** and immediately dodge. You cancel the skill animation (preserving its cooldown) but still gain the ultra-fast Schlacht charge.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flame Wheel Bug">
    Cast **Flame Wheel**, immediately charge **Schlacht**, release it, and **let go of your controller**. This associates Schlacht's massive damage cap with the Flame Wheel ticks.
  </Card>
