<Section icon={Swords} title="Zeta combo loop and skill timing">

Zeta’s strongest turns come from disciplined attack windows. You want to enter a fight with a plan: build pressure, move into your opening, spend your burst tools, then reposition before the boss starts swinging back. That is the difference between “busy” damage and effective damage.

The cleanest Zeta loop is not complicated, but it does demand patience. Use your mobility to stay close, wait for a committed boss animation, and fire your best tools when the target is least likely to move. In co-op, this also means you can line up with Link Attacks and Chain Bursts instead of burning everything at random.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set up safe pressure">
    Start with normal attacks and movement so you can read the boss. Do not force your biggest damage buttons immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Spend skills during openings">
    Use your highest-value offensive skill when the boss is locked in place, stunned, or recovering from a long animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stay mobile after burst">
    Reposition before the next pattern begins. Zeta is strongest when she keeps attacking without getting clipped out of her rotation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Sync party damage">
    Save major burst tools for Link Attack moments, Skybound Arts chains, and party-wide damage windows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Situation | Best Action | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Boss opening | Use burst skill, then extend pressure | Best damage per safe window |
| Boss moving | Reposition first | Prevents missed hits and wasted cooldowns |
| Link gauge ready | Chase Link Attack | Boosts total party output |
| Chain Burst opportunity | Hold Skybound Arts | Improves burst timing |
| Getting hit too often | Slow down and reset spacing | Keeps the run stable |

<Callout type="info" title="Timing Matters More Than Spam">
Zeta performs best when you treat her like a precision attacker. If you press everything on cooldown without reading the fight, you lose the very advantage that makes her worth building.
</Callout>
