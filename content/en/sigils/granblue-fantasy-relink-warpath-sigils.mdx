<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Warpath Sigils">

Acquiring Granblue Fantasy Relink Warpath Sigils requires a shift in focus toward the endgame boss, Behemoth. Unlike the standard character sigils found in the Knickknack Shop early on, Warpath variants require specific materials that only drop from high-level encounters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Locking Horns">
    Reach the Proud difficulty tier and complete the prerequisite quests to unlock the Behemoth encounter. This boss is the primary source of the materials needed for Warpath sigils.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Behemoth Materials">
    Defeat Behemoth to collect **Giant Beaks** and **Primordial Horns**. High-rank finishes (S++ or higher) increase the quantity and rarity of the drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Treasure Trade at Siero's">
    Visit Siero's Knickknack Shop and navigate to the Treasure Trade menu. Under the Sigils tab, you can exchange your Behemoth materials for the Warpath sigil specific to your character.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Material Management">
Do not waste your Behemoth drops on generic upgrades early on. Save them specifically for the Warpath sigils of your main party, as the drop rates for certain horns can be quite low.
</Callout>

Once you have secured your Granblue Fantasy Relink Warpath Sigils, the next step is "Awakening" your equipment. This process involves maxing out the sigil levels and pairing them with Wrightstones to add sub-stats like HP or Auto-Revive.

1. **Prioritize Damage Cap**: Even with a Warpath sigil, you will hit the damage ceiling quickly. Ensure you have at least 4 Damage Cap V sigils equipped.
2. **Stamina vs. Enmity**: Depending on your character's playstyle, pair your Warpath sigil with Stamina (for high health maintenance) or Enmity (for low health high-risk play).
3. **Utility Sub-stats**: Look for Sigil+ variants that offer Potion Hoarder or Aegis as secondary traits to improve survivability in Proud+ quests.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Over-Capping">
