<Section icon={Map} title="Optimal Combat Rotation and Mastery Priority">

The core gameplay loop of a **granblue fantasy relink fraux build** revolves around gauge management. You want to enter the **Strength Gains** state as often as possible to unleash her enhanced skills.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gauge Generation">
    Perform the basic attack combo into the **Flip Kick** finisher. This automatically swaps your stance and begins filling the alternate gauge without losing momentum.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Strength Gains Activation">
    Once both the Blue and Orange gauges are full, Strength Gains activates. Your next three skill casts will be enhanced and cost no cooldown.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enhanced Skill Cycle">
    Prioritize using **Dead Lands** for damage, **All Fall Down** for the DEF debuff, and **Indominus** if you need health or **Crimson Nightmare** for Slow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset and Repeat">
    After the three free casts, use the **Stance Flip** special attack to damage nearby enemies and immediately begin rebuilding your gauges.
  </Step>
</Steps>
