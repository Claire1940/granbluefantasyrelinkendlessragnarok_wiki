The Versales Heart sigil grants 10% enhanced damage in both Dragon and Godmight forms. While it increases the depletion rate of the gauge by 200%, it prevents Id from transforming back until the gauge is completely empty. This allows for multiple Godmight finishers in a single window, especially when paired with Link Time.

| Form Feature | Base Effect | With Versales Heart |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dragon Damage** | Standard Dark DMG | +10% Enhanced Damage |
| **Godmight Finishers** | Single use ends form | Multiple uses allowed until depletion |
| **Stout Heart** | Only in Dragon Form | Active in both transformed states |
| **Gauge Management** | Manual transitions | Auto-lock until empty |

<Callout type="warning" title="Transformation Timing">
If you do not have 4 stacks (ticks) by the time the Dragon Form gauge depletes, you will revert to Human form instead of entering Godmight. Always prioritize Link Attacks to ensure the transition.
</Callout>

- **Skill Refresh**: Use Ragnarok Form while in Godmight to fully restore the gauge, effectively doubling your time in the most powerful state.
