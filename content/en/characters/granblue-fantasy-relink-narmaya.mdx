The two stances define her basic attack strings and how she interacts with the enemy. Dawnfly stance is characterized by a pink flare and slower, wide-reaching slashes, while Freeflutter stance features a blue flare and rapid-fire, single-target strikes.

| Stance | Flare Color | Attack Speed | Primary Use | Finisher Type |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastering the Shift">
Never switch stances manually with the stance button while standing still. Always perform a Stance Shift by pressing the stance button at the end of a combo or after a skill to perform a powerful transition attack that grants butterflies.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Butterfly System and Combat Flow">

The core of **granblue fantasy relink narmaya** is the butterfly mechanic. You can hold up to six butterflies at once. These are not just visual flair; they significantly increase the damage and effect duration of her skills. Landing specific combo finishers, stance shift finishers, and link attacks grants these butterflies.

To optimize butterfly generation, you must understand the "Perfect Shift." For Dawnfly stance, the fastest way to charge the hold attack is to perform three normal attacks first. For Freeflutter, you simply spam the attack button until the windmill animation triggers, then shift.

| Action | Butterfly Gain | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stance Shift Finisher** | 1-2 | Requires perfect timing at the end of a combo |
| **Link Attack** | 1 | Allows for an immediate stance shift finisher |
| **Dance of Pink Petals** | 3 | Instant gain upon activation |
| **Dance of Blue Petals** | 3 | Instant gain plus defensive buffs |
| **Butterflies' Grace** | +1 | Bonus butterfly on every finisher (Sigil required) |
