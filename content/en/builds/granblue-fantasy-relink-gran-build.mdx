<Section icon={Target} title="The Optimal Combat Rotation">

Executing the perfect rotation is what separates a master Captain from a novice. The goal of a **granblue fantasy relink gran build** is to cycle through your skills as many times as possible within a 60-second window. In 2026, the standard "Training Dummy" rotation has been optimized for real combat scenarios where bosses move frequently.

**The Standard Rotation Flow:**
1.  Start with **Regan Leaf** to begin the cooldown immediately.
2.  Dump **Overdrive Surge** and **Armor Break**.
3.  Use **Decimate** last to benefit from the maximum cooldown reduction procs from Cascade.
4.  Perform the **X-X-Y-X-Y (Hold Y)** combo to reach Arts Level 4.
5.  Upon reaching Level 4, your skills (specifically Regan Leaf) should be nearly off cooldown.
6.  Cast **Regan Leaf** again immediately.
7.  Wait for the Arts Gauge to reset (approx. 6.5 seconds) and repeat.

| Rotation Phase | Action | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Opener** | Skill Dump | Initial burst and stun buildup |
| **Recovery** | Arts Combo | Recharge gauge and reduce cooldowns |
| **Peak** | Arts Level 4 | Maximize skill damage and CDR bonus |
| **Reset** | Gauge Depletion | Prepare for the next skill cycle |

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Tactic: Double Regan Leaf">
Because of the insane cooldown reduction at Arts Level 4, you can often cast **Regan Leaf** at Level 0, finish your combo to hit Level 4, and have **Regan Leaf** ready again before the Level 4 state expires. This "double-dipping" significantly boosts your overall DPS.
</Callout>
