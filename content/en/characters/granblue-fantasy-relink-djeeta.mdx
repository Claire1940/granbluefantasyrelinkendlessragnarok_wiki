<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Djeeta's Adept Arts Mechanic">

In the 2026 meta of **granblue fantasy relink djeeta** remains one of the most flexible characters due to her unique "Adept Arts" system. Unlike specialized units, Djeeta's effectiveness is directly tied to her Class Level. By successfully landing combo finishers, players raise their Arts level, which significantly boosts the potency, duration, or hit count of her equipped skills.

The rhythm of playing Djeeta involves a constant cycle of basic attacks into finishers to maintain a high Arts level before unleashing powerful abilities. This makes her gameplay both rewarding and strategically deep, as you must decide whether to use a skill early for immediate utility or wait for a higher Arts level for maximum impact.
