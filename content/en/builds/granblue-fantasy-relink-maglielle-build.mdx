### The Veil Gauge and Blade Dance

Unlike standard characters, Maglielle's basic three-hit attack combo does not generate gauge under normal circumstances. Instead, her gauge is tied directly to her unique triangle attack, Blade Dance. To fill the gauge, you must execute combo finishers.

| Finishers | Input Sequence | Gauge Gain | Playstyle Utility |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Combo A** | Square -> Triangle (Hold) | Moderate | Fastest to execute, highly reliable |
| **Combo B** | Square -> Square -> Triangle (Hold) | High | Balanced AoE and single-target damage |
| **Combo C** | Square -> Square -> Square -> Triangle (Hold) | Massive | Highest damage, hardest to aim |

To optimize your rotation, prioritize Combo A. Combos B and C have longer animations and are more difficult to aim manually in fast-paced encounters.

### Empowered Finishers (Pink Glow)

Holding down the Triangle button during any combo finisher allows you to manually aim the attack. Once the blades connect with the enemy, they turn from gold to a purple-pink hue. 

This visual change indicates that the attack is empowered. Landing these pink finishers grants significantly more Veil gauge and temporarily enhances your basic square attacks, allowing them to generate gauge as well.

<Callout type="tip" title="Gauge Refund Mechanic">
When channeling Blade Dance, letting go of the Triangle button roughly half a second after the gauge completely empties will refund up to a third of your total Veil gauge. Avoid releasing the button the exact millisecond it hits zero, or you will miss this crucial refund window.
</Callout>

### Choosing Your Fourth Skill Slot

While the first three skills are non-negotiable for a top-tier **granblue fantasy relink maglielle build**, your fourth slot should adapt to your playstyle or team needs:

- **Stave of Divinity**: Summons Caduceus, a mobile healing circle that automatically revives fallen allies. It is easily one of the best support skills in the game.
- **Knock Music**: A defensive back-step dodge that grants invincibility frames and generates massive gauge when timed perfectly against enemy attacks.
- **Senuutu**: Applies a personal shield. This should only be used if you specialize in the Sword Veil Guardianship master trait tree.

For your character-specific slots, prioritize **Blade Queen's Warpath** and **Blade Queen's Circuit**.
