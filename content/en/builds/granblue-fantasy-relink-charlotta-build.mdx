<Section icon={Target} title="How to Build Charlotta Step by Step">

Follow a simple order and the build comes together fast. Charlotta rewards players who upgrade in the right sequence, because her kit already gives you speed and pressure. Your job is to turn that into repeatable clears.

<Callout type="tip" title="Upgrade in This Order">
Get your weapon and core sigils online first, then refine damage and utility. That keeps every resource spend useful instead of wasteful.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pick one main weapon path">
    Use the best sword you can maintain comfortably, then keep investing in the weapon you are actually using. Do not spread materials across every option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Lock in basic survival">
    Add HP or defense if bosses are still punishing you. Charlotta benefits from staying alive long enough to keep pressure on target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build around uptime">
    Add cooldown help, consistent offense, and any trait that lets you keep attacking. Her strongest trait is tempo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Swap into endgame offense">
    When damage starts capping, move into Damage Cap, War Elemental, or Supplementary Damage if your account can support them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Tune for your mode">
    For solo play, add more safety. For co-op, lean harder into sustained damage and team-friendly consistency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

|---|---|---|
| Day One | Weapon level, easy sigils | Basic HP or attack |
| Midgame | Main party stability | Cooldown and utility |

| Hard Quests | Boss-specific comfort | Extra survival if needed |
