<Section icon={Zap} title="Mastering Shroud Marks and Combo Finishers">

The core of **granblue fantasy relink yodarha** gameplay is managing his three Shroud Marks. These marks are consumed by his skills to enhance their effects, but they are also the key to his "Infinite Loop" mechanic. When you perform a combo finisher, Yodarha becomes invincible for the duration of the animation, making the aggressive pursuit of finishers both an offensive and defensive necessity.

### The Shroud Mark Scaling
Each skill in Yodarha's kit behaves differently based on the number of marks currently active. Understanding these thresholds is vital for maximizing DPS.

| Skill Name | 0 Marks Effect | 3 Marks Effect | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Awakening** | ~350k Damage | ~700k Damage | Gap closer and fast mark builder |
| **Empty Mist** | Short Duration | Maximum Slashes | Highest burst damage (3.2M+) |
| **Flashing Void** | Single Lunge | 12-Hit Combo | High damage with movement control |
| **Sky Shatter** | Base Impact | Max Beam Damage | Quick access to invincibility |
| **Tit for Tat** | Standard Parry | Enhanced Counter | Defensive utility with damage |

### Unlocking the Infinite Loop
To reach Yodarha's peak performance, you must trigger his special combo state. By landing four different combo finishers in succession, his basic combo string shortens significantly. This allows you to spam the final, invincible portion of his attack string with just a single button press. This state persists as long as you continue attacking, turning Yodarha into a whirlwind of steel that is nearly impossible for enemies to hit.

<Callout type="warning" title="Combo Maintenance">
The infinite loop ends if you stop attacking or take a significant pause. Always use your gap-closers like **Awakening** to stay glued to the boss and keep the chain alive.
</Callout>

To maximize your damage window, especially during Link Time or when a boss is broken, follow this specific sequence:

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Defensive Tactics and Countering">

Yodarha is often called "Immortal" not just because of his Mirror Images, but because of his unique heavy attack counter. By holding the heavy attack button, Yodarha enters a blocking stance. Releasing the button exactly when a white circle flashes rewards you with a successful parry and a **Supplementary Damage** buff for 10 seconds.

### Mastering the Parry
Unlike other characters who rely on simple timing, Yodarha's parry requires you to *release* the button rather than press it. This allows for a more controlled defensive playstyle.
