<Section icon={Shield} title="The Perfected Undying Loop Build">

The most popular and highly effective end-game setup for Vaseraga is the Undying Loop build. This playstyle centers around the skill **Immortal Pain**, which grants debuff immunity and the Undying buff, preventing Vaseraga's health from falling below 1 HP. 

The primary challenge of Immortal Pain is its long cooldown and the fact that it drops Vaseraga to 1 HP when the effect ends. To overcome this, the build utilizes his signature sigil, **Ebony Poise**. This sigil reduces skill cooldowns by up to 8% whenever a fully charged attack lands. When paired with Supplementary Damage sigils, each hit registers twice, effectively doubling the cooldown reduction and allowing you to reactivate Immortal Pain before the previous buff expires.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Combo Canceling Techniques">

Because Vaseraga's standard animations are slow, mastering animation cancels is essential to maximizing your damage per second (DPS). There are two primary cancel methods that players use to bypass slow setup animations and reach heavy charged finishers almost instantly.

The first is the **Skill Dodge Cancel**. By activating a skill like Great Scythe of Groth and immediately dodging, you cancel the skill's animation but retain the ability to transition directly into a fully charged heavy combo finisher. Crucially, because the skill was canceled before completion, it does not go on cooldown, allowing you to repeat this loop indefinitely.

The second method is the **Light Attack Dodge Cancel**, which is highly effective for builds that do not rely on skill usage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Light Attack">
    Perform your first basic light attack to start the combo sequence.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Dodge Cancel">
    Dodge immediately before the swing finishes to cancel the recovery frames.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Repeat the Cancel">
    Perform a second light attack and immediately cancel it with another dodge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash Heavy Finisher">
    Hold the heavy attack button to immediately begin charging your most powerful finisher, skipping the slow build-up phases entirely.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Practice Timing">
Spend time in the training area to master the rhythm of the dodge cancels. Canceling too early will prevent the combo sequence from registering, while canceling too late defeats the purpose of saving time.
</Callout>
