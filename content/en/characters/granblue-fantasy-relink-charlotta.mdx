**Essential Sigils for 2026:**
1. **Holy Knight's Grandeur:** This is her character-specific Sigil. It extends the duration of Noble Stance and provides a significant boost to her finisher damage.
2. **Damage Cap V+:** You will need four of these to reach the maximum level (Level 65).
3. **War Elemental:** Converts all damage to the element the enemy is weak to, providing a flat 20% damage increase.
4. **Tyranny / Life on the Line:** These provide massive raw attack boosts at the cost of health or healing, which is acceptable since Charlotta should be parrying or dodging.

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Noble Stance Combo">

Playing Charlotta effectively requires understanding her "loop." She is not a button-masher; she is a rhythmic fighter. Follow these steps to maximize your uptime in Noble Stance.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Combo">
    Start with your basic Square (X on Xbox) combo. Once the yellow aura appears around Charlotta, you have entered the preliminary phase of Noble Stance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Noble Transition">
    Press Triangle (Y on Xbox) after a full basic combo to perform a Noble Finisher. This move has high stagger value and transitions you into the high-speed attack mode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain the Loop">
    While in Noble Stance, keep attacking. If the boss moves, use **Shining Onslaught** to close the gap without dropping your combo counter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Parry Reset">
    If an attack is incoming, use her built-in parry (Triangle/Y during an enemy hit). A successful parry allows you to immediately counter-attack and stay in Noble Stance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Advanced Tech">
You can "buffer" your Noble Stance attacks. If you jump and attack, you can land directly into a Noble Finisher, skipping the standard wind-up time.
</Callout>
