### How to Build the Ares Gauge
The Ares Gauge is divided into two halves [sCRz5ewzt1k]. Because of a hard threshold at the 50% mark, you cannot fill the gauge past halfway with a single combo finisher [sCRz5ewzt1k]. This means you must weave multiple short combos to reach 100% [sCRz5ewzt1k].

| Combo Input | Gauge Gain | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **X -> Y** (Short Finisher) | Minor | Quick top-off when near thresholds [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **X -> X -> Y** (Mid Finisher) | 25% | Standard neutral poke [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **X -> X -> X -> Y** (Full Finisher) | 50% (Maxes at threshold) | Main gauge builder during downtime [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **Link Attack** | 25%+ | Instant gauge gain during combat [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **Skill Finisher** | 50% | High-commitment gauge builder [sCRz5ewzt1k] |

### Maintaining Ares Uptime
Once Ares is summoned, he will immediately disappear unless you chain specific actions [sCRz5ewzt1k]. To keep Ares active indefinitely, you must loop your offensive abilities in a tight sequence [sCRz5ewzt1k]. 

| Action Type | Keeps Ares Active? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Damaging Skills** | Yes | Primary chain starter [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **Ares Pact Strike (Y-Spam)** | Yes | Core damage loop [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **Link Attacks** | Yes | Extends combo windows [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **Skybound Art (SBA)** | Yes | Must chain into a skill immediately after [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **Utility Skills** | No | Will drop Ares unless immediately cancelled into an attack [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
| **Dodging / Knockbacks** | No | Instantly drops Ares; avoid unless absolutely necessary [sCRz5ewzt1k] |
