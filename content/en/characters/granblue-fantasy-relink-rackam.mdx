### Attack Patterns and Timing

Rackam's basic combo involves rapid-fire shots with his rifle. You can choose to mash the button for a steady stream of bullets or time your shots for better accuracy and heat control.

| Attack Name | Input | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bull's Eye Blast** | Hold Heavy Attack | High-damage charged shot. Speed increases with Heat. |
| **Rapid Fire** | Tap Light Attack | Standard gunshots. Fast SBA building. |
| **Aerial Shot** | Jump + Light | Useful for dodging ground AOEs while maintaining DPS. |
| **Slide Shot** | Dodge + Light | Repositions Rackam while dealing chip damage. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Overheating">
In the 2026 meta, "Overheating" is a massive DPS loss. Always aim to fire your Bull's Eye Blast when the gauge is at roughly 80-90% to reset the meter and maximize damage output.
</Callout>
