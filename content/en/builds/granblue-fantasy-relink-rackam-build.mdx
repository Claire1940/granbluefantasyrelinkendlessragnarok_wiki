<Section icon={Swords} title="Rackam Skill Loadout and Combat Loop">

Rackam’s skill plan should support a simple loop: fire safely, spend skills during openings, then reset before the next boss pattern. You want a loadout that handles long fights without forcing you into risky positioning.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open from range">
    Start fights at a safe distance, keep the camera stable, and look for clean lines of fire before committing skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Spend skills on real openings">
    Use stronger abilities when the boss is locked in an animation, recovering, stunned, or grouped with adds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Convert burst windows">
    Save your hardest-hitting tools for Link Attacks, Chain Bursts, and other moments where the party can amplify damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset position fast">
    After the burst, move early. Rackam gains value when he keeps attacking instead of standing still and getting clipped.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Skill Slot | Best Role | What It Should Do |
|---|---|---|
| Slot 1 | Primary burst | Deliver your most reliable damage spike |
| Slot 2 | Consistent pressure | Keep damage flowing during moderate openings |
| Slot 3 | Mobility or safety | Help you reposition without losing tempo |
| Slot 4 | Flex utility | Cover cooldown gaps, control, or extra burst |
