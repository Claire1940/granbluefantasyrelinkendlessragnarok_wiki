<Section icon={Target} title="How to Acquire the Less Is More Sigil">

Acquiring the **granblue fantasy relink less is more sigil** is different from farming standard Sigils like Damage Cap V or Critical Hit Rate V. You cannot simply complete a quest and expect it to drop as a direct reward. Instead, you must engage with the Curio system, which acts as a "loot box" mechanic managed by Zathba and Sierokarte.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Farm Curios">
    Complete high-level quests (Maniac or Proud difficulty) to earn Curios. These appear as purple orbs in the reward screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit the Second Town">
    Travel to Seedhollow and locate Zathba's bar or Sierokarte’s Knick-knack Shack to begin the appraisal process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Appraise and Reset">
    Appraise your Curios. Note that Less is More is a rare drop alongside other exclusive sigils like War Elemental and Glass Cannon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transmarvel Alternative">
    While Less is More is Curio-exclusive, use your leftover materials to perform Level 3 Transmutations to build your Transmarvel gauge for other essential endgame sigils.
  </Step>
</Steps>
