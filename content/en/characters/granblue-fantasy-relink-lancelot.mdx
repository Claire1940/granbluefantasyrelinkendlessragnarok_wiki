In high-level Maniac and Proud difficulty quests, such as fighting the Inferno Dial, Lancelot's role is to maintain constant pressure. While other characters might have to back off during "Overdrive" phases, Lancelot can stay glued to the boss's side.

**Boss Combat Flow:**
1. **Engage**: Use Triangle dash to close the gap instantly.
2. **Burst**: Activate Southern Cross and follow up with a full Square combo.
3. **Control**: When the boss prepares a major AOE, use **Kaltzwinger** to freeze them, allowing the team to continue DPS.
4. **Evade**: If the boss uses a multi-hit ground slam, use **Turbulence** to stay in the air and continue the assault.
5. **Support**: Share your SBA gauge if you reach 100% significantly faster than your teammates to trigger a Full Burst.
