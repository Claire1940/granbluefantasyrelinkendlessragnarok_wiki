<Section icon={Trophy} title="Reaching the Damage Cap Level 65">

The most common mistake players make with **granblue fantasy relink yodarha sigils** is over-investing in raw Attack power while neglecting the Damage Cap. In Granblue Fantasy: Relink, every attack has a ceiling. Once you hit that ceiling, adding more Attack sigils does nothing. You must increase the ceiling itself.

| Component | Level Contribution | Total Cap Increase |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4x Damage Cap V+ Sigils** | Level 60 | +210% |
| **Terminus Weapon (Maxed)** | Level 1 (Sigil Booster) | +100% (Base trait) |
| **Over Mastery Rolls** | Variable | +20% |
| **Total Target** | **Level 65** | **+250%** |

To reach the maximum level of 65, you need four Level 15 Damage Cap sigils. The Terminus weapon's **Sigil Booster** trait will then push these to Level 16 each, totaling Level 64. The final level or additional percentage points usually come from your **Over Mastery** stats, which you should roll at the Knickknack Shack.
