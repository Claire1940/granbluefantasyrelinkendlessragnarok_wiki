To maximize your damage as a **granblue fantasy relink io** main, you must master the "Quick Cast" mechanic [1, 2]. Quick casting significantly increases Stargaze's charge speed when executed immediately after gaining a Mystic Vortex orb [1, 2].

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Generate Orbs">
    Cast a spell (like Fire or Freeze) or land a fully charged normal attack to generate Mystic Vortex orbs [1, 2].
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger Quick Cast">
    Immediately press and hold your Stargaze button right after the orb-generating action finishes [1, 2]. You will see a distinct visual indicator showing Stargaze charging much faster [1, 2].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unleash Stargaze Level 4">
    Release the Stargaze attack once it reaches Level 4 (consuming 4 orbs for maximum damage) [1, 2].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dodge Cancel">
    The instant the magic circle appears and Stargaze fires, dodge in any direction to cancel the recovery animation and reposition [1].
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Link Time Optimization">

Your optimal rotation during this window is to simply alternate between a fast spell (Fire or Freeze) and a Quick Cast Stargaze repeatedly [1]. If your cooldowns are not fast enough, use a quick charged attack to bridge the gap [1].
