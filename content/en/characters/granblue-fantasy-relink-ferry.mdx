### Core Combat Mechanics
Ferry's combat loop revolves around summoning her pets to fight alongside her [70N5yfbBe-M, Ferry - Granblue Fantasy Relink Wiki]. Her basic attack chain summons a pet on the final hit, while her heavy attack (Onslaught) unleashes a massive multi-hit flurry [70N5yfbBe-M, Ferry - Granblue Fantasy Relink Wiki]. However, Onslaught dismisses all active pets at the end of the animation [70N5yfbBe-M, Ferry - Granblue Fantasy Relink Wiki].

| Ability Type | Input Command | Pet Interaction | Main Benefit |

| **Basic Combo** | Hold Square | Summons 1 pet on final hit | Safe poke, builds resources [70N5yfbBe-M, Ferry - Granblue Fantasy Relink Wiki] |
| **Onslaught** | Hold Triangle | Dismisses all active pets | High backloaded burst damage [70N5yfbBe-M, Ferry - Granblue Fantasy Relink Wiki] |
| **Link Attack** | Prompt Trigger | Summons all pets instantly | Instant setup for big damage [70N5yfbBe-M, Ferry - Granblue Fantasy Relink Wiki] |
| **Aerial Combo** | Jump + Square | No pet interaction | High safety, quick recovery [70N5yfbBe-M, Ferry - Granblue Fantasy Relink Wiki] |
