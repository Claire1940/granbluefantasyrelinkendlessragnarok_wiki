<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Loop and Burst Windows">

Seofon plays best when you follow a repeatable loop: build momentum, spend resources in the right window, then reset before the boss punishes you. That sounds basic, but basic execution is what keeps him strong.

<Callout type="success" title="Core combat idea">
Use normal pressure to create openings, then spend skills when the enemy is locked down, staggered, or busy finishing a long animation.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open with safe pressure">
    Start with your most reliable attack string and watch the boss. The goal is to establish momentum without forcing a bad trade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Save the expensive tools">
    Hold your strongest skills for openings. Seofon gets more value from one safe burst than from three panic buttons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Convert stagger into damage">
    When the boss is stunned or locked in place, spend your burst tools immediately. This is where your build actually pays off.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset before danger returns">
    If the pattern becomes unstable, disengage, dodge, and re-enter on a cleaner beat. Surviving the cycle matters more than forcing damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Situation | Best Action | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Boss opening | Use a safe string first | Build pressure without risk |
| Stagger window | Spend skills and burst | Highest damage conversion |
| Erratic phase | Reposition and wait | Avoid wasted HP |
| Party burst | Sync with team attacks | Better co-op output |

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Better Habit |
|---|---|---|
| Spamming skills on cooldown | Burns damage into unsafe frames | Save skills for openings |
| Chasing every hit | Causes avoidable damage taken | Reset and re-enter cleanly |
| Ignoring team burst tools | Lowers total party damage | Align with Link Attacks |
| Overbuilding offense early | Leaves weak survivability | Balance power and safety |

<Callout type="tip" title="Practical rotation advice">
If a fight feels messy, slow your rhythm down. Seofon is strongest when the player is calm, not when the screen is loud.
</Callout>
