<Section icon={Shield} title="Active and AFK Farming Routes">

To fuel your Transmarvel rolls, you need a steady stream of materials to trade for vouchers. Depending on your playstyle, you can choose between highly efficient manual boss runs or automated AFK setups.

### Active Farming: The Blazing Trial (Proud)
The Blazing Trial pits you against the Fire Dragon. Despite being a Proud difficulty boss, it has a relatively low health pool and predictable attack patterns.
- **Average Clear Time**: Under 2 minutes with AI companions.
- **Key Drops**: High-tier fire materials (worth massive voucher points) and Silver Centrum.
- **Strategy**: Target and break the horns early to secure bonus drop rewards.

### AFK Farming: General Investigation Galanza (Extreme)
If you want to farm while away from your system, Galanza's arena is the perfect target. Because the arena is small, Galanza stays in range of your characters constantly.
- **Average Clear Time**: 20 to 30 seconds.
- **Method**: Enable Full Assist Mode in your gameplay settings, select a ranged character like Rackam or Eugen, and let the AI fight.
- **Limitation**: The game limits consecutive auto-repeats to 10 runs before requiring manual town entry.
