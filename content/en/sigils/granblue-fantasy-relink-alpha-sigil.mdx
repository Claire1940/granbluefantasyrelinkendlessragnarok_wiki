<Section icon={Target} title="How to Decide Whether to Keep It Equipped">

Granblue Fantasy Relink rewards practical testing more than blind stat chasing. The official character-building manual and systems page are the best references for broader build systems: [Character Building manual](https://relink.granbluefantasy.jp/en/manual/detail?s=character_building) and [Endless Ragnarok systems](https://relink-ragnarok.granbluefantasy.com/en/systems/). Use those layers as your baseline, then judge the sigil in the quest tier you actually farm.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Match the sigil to your role">
    Decide whether your character is a burst dealer, ranged attacker, tanky frontline, or support pick. A sigil that fits the role is usually stronger than a generic stat boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Compare real gains, not tooltip hype">
    Ask whether the sigil improves damage uptime, safety, or team tempo. If the difference is tiny, the slot may be better used elsewhere.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Test it in your hardest stable quest">
    Run the sigil in a quest you can clear consistently. If it only feels good in easy content, it may not be worth the investment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Keep it only if it helps multiple fights">
    A strong sigil should work across bosses, not just one matchup. The more often it matters, the better its long-term value.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Situation | Keep the Alpha Sigil | Replace It |
|---|---|---|
| Bosses punish mistakes | Yes, if it improves survival | If it only adds tiny offense |
| You already clear fast | Yes, if it raises consistency | If it does not change outcomes |
| Your build lacks utility | Yes, if it fills a gap | If another sigil covers the same job |
| You are entering harder quests | Yes, if it helps stability | If it is just a placeholder |

<Callout type="warning" title="Upgrade Warning">
Do not overinvest rare materials into a sigil that only looks good on paper. In Relink, bad spending slows the whole party's growth.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Farming and Upgrade Priorities">

The easiest way to waste time is farming the wrong layer first. Relink progression works best when you separate early comfort, postgame power, and 2026 expansion goals. That means building your weapon path, then your sigils, then your high-end optimization.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Finish the current content tier first">
    Clear the quest tier you can handle reliably. Farming below your real level slows progression, and farming too high too early creates expensive failures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade the weapon before chasing perfect sigils">
    A stronger weapon gives immediate value. If your weapon is behind, even a good sigil will feel underwhelming.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use sigils that solve a current problem">
    Prioritize offense, survival, or cooldown support based on the quest type. Do not chase a final build before your core setup is stable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Move into premium endgame slots later">
    Once your character is consistent, shift toward Damage Cap, War Elemental, Supplementary Damage, and other late-game pieces.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Rebuild after major 2026 updates">
    Endless Ragnarok adds new systems such as Sigil Synthesis, Master Traits, summons, Chaos difficulty, and Conflux, so revisit old setups instead of keeping outdated habits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Farming Note">
The official product pages are worth bookmarking for long-term planning: the [base game site](https://relink.granbluefantasy.jp/en/) and the [Endless Ragnarok expansion page](https://relink-ragnarbluefantasy.com/en/). If a build path changes, those pages are the safest place to verify it.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid This Mistake">

</Callout>
