<Section icon={Target} title="How to Build the Party Step by Step">

The safest way to build a strong team is to lock in one job at a time. Start with your main character, then add the pieces that make that character better in real quests. That keeps material waste low and helps your team grow in a way that matches the content you are actually clearing.

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Logic">
Build for consistency first, then improve damage. A steady team clears more quests, earns more materials, and reaches the good sigil and weapon pieces faster than a team built only for screenshots.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose one main character">
    Pick the character you can pilot cleanly. Captain, Lancelot, Eugen, Cagliostro, and Vane are easy to build around if you want a lower-friction start.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Cover the missing role">
    Add a frontline if your team gets knocked around, a ranged attacker if bosses move too much, or a support slot if healing and buffs are lacking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade weapons for the active four">
    Keep the main party's weapon levels moving together. Do not overinvest in characters you are not bringing into quests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Tune sigils around the fight">
    Use simple offensive and defensive sigils first. Then replace them with higher-value pieces as your quest rank rises and your build starts demanding more precision.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Recheck the party after tough quests">
    After a difficult clear or failure, ask whether the problem was damage, survival, or boss control. Fix the missing role before changing everything else.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Upgrade priorities by phase**

| Phase | Priority | Good Team Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Early story | Comfort and survival | Captain, Lancelot, Eugen, Cagliostro |
| Midgame | Role balance | One DPS, one frontline, one ranged, one utility |
| Late game | Uptime and burst windows | Sandalphon, Seofon, Tweyen, Vane |
| Expansion era | Specialization | Beatrix, Eustace, Fraux, Fediel |
