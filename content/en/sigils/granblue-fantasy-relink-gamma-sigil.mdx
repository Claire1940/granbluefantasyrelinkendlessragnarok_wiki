<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm Gamma Sigils and Materials">

Acquiring the **granblue fantasy relink gamma sigil** is not a simple task. It requires consistent farming of the game's hardest encounter: Lucilius in "The Final Vision." This fight tests your team's coordination and your character's survival capabilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Final Vision">
    You must first complete the main story and reach the Zegagrande Legend rank. Lucilius is unlocked after completing specific Proud-difficulty quests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat Lucilius">
    The boss drops two primary materials: Tears of the Apocalypse and Dark Residue. Tears are the rarer drop, often yielding 0 to 3 per successful run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trade at Siero's Knickknack Shop">
    Navigate to the Treasure Trade menu. Under the Sigils tab, you can exchange your Lucilius drops for the Gamma+ sigil.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade at the Blacksmith">
    Once purchased, use Refinement materials and additional Dark Residue to level the sigil from 1 to 15 to unlock its full potential.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Drop Rate Variance">
